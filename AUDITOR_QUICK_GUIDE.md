# 👨‍🌾 Auditor Quick Guide - Offline Mode

## 🎯 Complete Audits Without Internet!

Your NuruOS app works **100% offline**. Here's how:

---

## 📱 **3-Step Process**

### **1️⃣ OFFLINE: Complete Audit**
```
📍 Open app (no internet needed)
📝 Fill out farm/business audit form
📷 Take photos with camera
🎤 Record voice notes
💾 Click "Save Draft"

✅ Everything saves to your phone!
```

### **2️⃣ OFFLINE: Review & Edit**
```
👀 View saved audits
✏️  Edit any draft
📊 Check completion status
💾 Save changes

✅ All changes saved locally!
```

### **3️⃣ ONLINE: Sync to Cloud**
```
📡 Connect to WiFi
🔄 Click "Sync" button
⏳ Wait for "Synced" ✅
📊 Check Google Sheets

✅ All audits uploaded!
```

---

## 💡 **Simple Rules**

### ✅ **DO:**
- Complete audits offline
- Take as many photos as needed
- Save drafts frequently
- Sync when you have good WiFi

### ❌ **DON'T:**
- Uninstall app before syncing
- Clear browser data before syncing
- Worry about internet in the field
- Wait to sync - batch multiple audits

---

## 🔋 **Field Checklist**

**Before leaving office:**
- [ ] Phone fully charged
- [ ] App installed on home screen
- [ ] Open app once (caches files)
- [ ] Test camera/microphone

**In the field:**
- [ ] Complete audits normally
- [ ] Save each audit
- [ ] Check "Pending sync" count

**Back at office:**
- [ ] Connect to WiFi
- [ ] Click "Sync"
- [ ] Verify in Google Sheets
- [ ] Done! ✅

---

## 📊 **Sync Status Guide**

| Icon | Status | Meaning | Action |
|------|--------|---------|--------|
| ⏸️ | **Draft** | Not finished | Continue editing |
| ⏳ | **Pending** | Waiting to sync | Connect to WiFi |
| ✅ | **Synced** | Uploaded | Done! |
| ❌ | **Failed** | Error | Will auto-retry |

---

## 🚨 **Quick Fixes**

### **Storage Full?**
→ Sync audits to free space

### **Can't Save?**
→ Close other apps, try again

### **Sync Failed?**
→ Check internet, wait 5 min, auto-retries

### **Lost Audit?**
→ Check "Drafts" - it's saved!

---

## 💾 **How Much Can I Store?**

**Typical:**
- 1 audit + 5 photos = **2-3 MB**
- Can store **100+ audits** offline
- Sync clears photos, keeps data

**Your phone storage:**
- Check: Settings → Storage
- Need: 500 MB free minimum
- Recommended: 1 GB free

→ No problem! Data auto-saved
